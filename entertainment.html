Jump Jive an' Wail

Jump Jive an' Wail is the best night way to spend a night jamming to the top jazz groups on the island. You'll be dancing better than Danny Zuko when you come here. Drinks, food, and dancing is the best way to pass the night in Taniti City!

Zipline Adventures

Zipline Adventures is all for seeking a thrilling trip while seeing all the beautiful nature of Taniti. Throw up a Shaka Bruh and say "Zipline!".

Halliday's Heaven

Halliday's Heaven is the biggest retro Arcade in the world! Sporting all of your arcade desires. This 3 story arcade comes with a bar, snack bar, and lounge area for when you need to rest the joystick wrist from a long bout with Tetris.
