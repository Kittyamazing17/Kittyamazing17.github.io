Beaches

Taniti is home to the most gorgeous beaches. These areas are perfect for setting up an umbrella, having a cool drink, and swimming in the crisp waters of the Pacific. There are so many to choose from that no one beach will ever become too crowded, even in peak season.

Hiking

Taniti has an active volcano, which can be hiked to with a tour group. This gallavant up the side of a lava mountain will end with a perfect view of Taniti City, and the rainforest. This hike will take a group an entire day, with meals included in the ticket price. It is recommend to bring hiking gear to the island if you plan to climb the mountain.

Boat Tours

Tour the beautiful seas around the island with your own personal or group boat tour. Vessels range in size from a small one mast sail boat all the way to a modern yacht. Spend your day fishing, drinking, and getting a solid tan! This trip last the entire day, and normally include meals, but this differs on the boat tour.
