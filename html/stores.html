Convenience Stores

Taniti has one Convenience store open 24 hours a day within the downtown of Taniti City! Here you can buy a mix of drinks, snack, and any items you may have forgotten before your trip!

Local Stores

Taniti has a few local markets for buying local foods, electronics, and clothes. The freshest fruits on the island come from these small spots, and will give you a taste of the island. Support your local business.

Supermarket

There are two large supermarkets on the island in Taniti City. This is where you can buy food from all over the world, though the prices will be more expensive than your use to back in your home country. They also sell anything you may need during your stay on the island.
