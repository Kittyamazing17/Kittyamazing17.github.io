Francis' Freshest

Taniti is home to three american-style restaurants, and cooks up the best burgers this side of the Serengeti. Francis' Freshest is the most popular burger restaurant on the island. Serving up the large portions locals and tourist alike crave. Don't forget to try Francis' Fire Fingers!

Dragonroll Fusion

Taniti is known for Asian Fusion food, and at Dragonroll Fusion the flavor is cracked up to 11! Whether you're in the mood for spicy fusion rolls, or a simple take on global cuisine, come to Dragonroll Fusion for all of your favorite treats acrossed the Pacific.

Slammin' Salmon Sashimi

Taniti is simply the best place to find fresh fish for fair prices. This is showcased within the Slammin' Salmon Sashimi. Known for their Tanitian take on traditional Japanese sashimi, this place will knock your sandals off into orbit. Just know that once you eat at the Slammin' there is no going back to supermarket sashimi.
