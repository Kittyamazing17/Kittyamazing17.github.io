Hostels

Taniti Island has a handful of hostels to fit the traveler looking to meet new people, save a few coins, and get a local experience on this island of paradise. Come to the best kept secrets of Taniti here.

Hotels

Taniti Island is home to many fine hotels ranging from 1 to 3 star accomodations. These hotels can be found within the city limits, or within the wilds of the rainforest. Whatever price, whatever location, Taniti will make sure to find what you need!

Royal Ripley's Resort

Royal Ripley's Resort is the greatest 4-star resort on in the Pacific, boasting luxurious hall, decadent decor, and the finest linens to rest your head. Rip in the royalty at this stunning high-society snobbery.
